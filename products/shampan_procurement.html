Shampan Procurement Management

Shampan Procurement Management manages the purchasing process from Requisition to Purchase Bill creation with multiple approval system in each stage. Based on Purchase Budget, each purchase requisition to Purchase Bill is approved by different authority level so that purchase control is in place. Select the best quotation from multiple Quotations to raise a single Purchase Order. You can track down your Purchase Budget variance, work completion status, Printing of Requisition to Purchase Bill with attachments of source documents for your complete audit history.Moreover, you can track your Product & Service Inventory in efficiently.

  • Common Service

    1. Branch & Department Settings
    2. Multi Currency with Conversion
    3. Unit of Measurement with Conversion
    4. Locking Fiscal Period & Year
    5. Email alerts reminder to approval authority for PR, PO, GRN & Invoice

  • Request for Quotation (RFQ)

    1. Create Request for Quotation based on multiple Purchase Requisition
    2. Reviewing All/Specific User’s/Completed RFQ

  • Purchasing Quotation Analysis

    1. Create unlimited Vendor Quotation based on RFQ
    2. Analysis of multiple vendor Quotations and select single Quotation.

    • Good Receive Note (GRN)

  • GRN is a document used to record the inward entry of the any goods received
  • The document normally consists of the details of Quantity Received, Rejected and Accepted, Supplier Name & P.O. No.
  • Preparing GRNs is important as it promotes proper inventory control and restricts the unwanted, unauthorized entry of goods.
  • The GRN preparation is a part of effective Inventory Control Management
